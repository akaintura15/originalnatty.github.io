STEP 1: CALCULATE YOUR BMR(Basal metabolic rate Even when resting, your body burns calories by performing basic functions to sustain life, such as:
- breathing
- circulation
- nutrient processing
- cell production
Basal metabolic rate is the number of calories your body needs to accomplish its most basic (basal) life-sustaining functions.

STEP 2: Gain weight , eat (100-200)kcal in surplus of bmr
lose weight, eat (200-250)kcal in deficit of bmr.
